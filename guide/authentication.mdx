## Overview

NoteMV uses Google Sign-In for secure authentication. This guide walks you through the login process, session management, and logout.

## Signing In with Google

<Steps>
  <Step title="Launch NoteMV">
    Open NoteMV in your browser. You'll be greeted with the 3D welcome scene.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Click 'Enter' or Sign In">
    Click the "Enter" button on the welcome screen to initiate the Google authentication flow.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Select Your Google Account">
    A popup window will appear asking you to:
    - Choose your Google account
    - Grant NoteMV permission to access your basic profile information (name, email, profile picture)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Wait for Authentication">
    You'll see a loading message: "ESTABLECIENDO ENLACE SEGURO..." (Establishing Secure Link)
    
    Once authenticated, you'll be automatically redirected to your dashboard.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
NoteMV uses Firebase Authentication with Google OAuth 2.0, ensuring your credentials are never stored on our servers.
</Tip>

## Session Management

Your session is automatically maintained by Firebase Authentication:

- **Persistent Sessions**: You remain logged in even after closing the browser
- **Secure Tokens**: Authentication tokens are refreshed automatically in the background
- **Multi-Device**: You can access NoteMV from multiple devices with the same account

### What Happens After Login

Once authenticated, NoteMV stores your user information:

```javascript
// User object structure
```

This information is used to:
- Identify your notes and projects
- Display your profile in collaboration features

## Logging Out

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open User Menu">
    Click your profile picture or name in the top-right corner of the dashboard.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Select 'Logout'">
    Click the "Cerrar Sesión" (Logout) button in the dropdown menu.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Confirm Logout">
    You'll be immediately signed out and returned to the welcome screen.
    
    All your data remains securely stored in the cloud.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
Logging out will:
- End your current session
- Clear authentication tokens from your browser
- Return you to the welcome screen

Your notes and projects remain safe and will be available when you sign back in.
</Warning>

## Troubleshooting

### Authentication Fails

If you encounter issues signing in:

1. **Check Popup Blockers**: Ensure your browser allows popups for NoteMV
2. **Clear Browser Cache**: Sometimes cached data can interfere with authentication
3. **Try Incognito Mode**: This rules out browser extension conflicts
4. **Check Google Account Status**: Ensure your Google account is active and accessible

### Session Expired

If your session expires (rare):

- You'll be automatically redirected to the login screen
- Simply sign in again to restore your session
- All your data will be immediately accessible
